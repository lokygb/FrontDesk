Evaluation

Feedback Sources
  • Instructors
  • TAs
  • Students
  • Independent sources
Collection Strategy
  • TA/Instructor Side
    • Survey the time spent grading and permanently recording grades
    • Survey the time spent creating tests
    • Survey the ability to give feedback
    • Survey the ability and effectiveness to look at student data
    • Survey the quality of the system, reliability, flexibility, usability
  • Student Side
    • Survey the amount and usefulness of feedback
    • Survey the usefulness of the system in catching carless mistakes
    • Survey the increase in communication with TAs and speed of grading results
    • Survey the quality of the system, reliability, flexibility, usability
Expected Results
  • Expected to show that the system provides an increased amount of feedback, decreased time spent grading and creating tests, and increased ability to view the results and statistics of assignment submissions.
  • Does the system get in the way of the class experience or facillitate it?
  • Does the system improve the ability to create tests easily or hinder it?
  • Does the system improve the ability to examine statistics of a sections assignment submissions?
  • Does the system increase the amount of communication and feedback between students and teaching assistants/instructors?
